Birth Certificate Instructions

  • Click on the Birth Certificate Box
  • Ensure that names, birth dates, and parent information match the documents.
  • AI assistance will highlight missing or incorrect information in red.
  • Only upload clear, scanned copies of the required documents.
  • Allowed file formats: JPEG, PNG, PDF (Max 5MB per file).
  • The system will generate an AI Summary of the application, highlighting key details.
  • Any missing or incorrect information will be shown for correction before submission.
  • Click On Apply for final submission.
  • Alternatively, click Save as Draft if you need to complete it later.

Required Documents:

  • Birth Certificate from the hospital
  • Statement of birth from the hospital or birthplace
  • Parent's marriage certificate
  • Parent's Identification Documents
  • Aadhaar card of parents
